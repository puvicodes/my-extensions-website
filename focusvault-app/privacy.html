Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how FocusVault ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information. We are committed to your privacy and transparency.

1. Data We Collect

FocusVault for Android stores your app block list, focus schedules, and session history locally on your device. No personal data is transmitted to external servers.

2. How We Use Data

Any data stored locally is used solely to provide and improve the core functionality of FocusVault. We do not sell, rent, or share any user data with third parties.

3. Permissions

This app requires usage access permission to monitor which apps are in use (in order to block them during focus sessions). It also requires the ability to display overlays to show blocking screens.

4. Third-Party Services

This App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Your usage remains completely private.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review it periodically.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at puvify@duck.com.
