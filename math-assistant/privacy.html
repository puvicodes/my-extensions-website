Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Math Assistant Pro ("the App") handles your data. We are committed to ensuring your privacy and security.

1. Information We Collect

Math Assistant Pro does not collect or store any personal identification information. All mathematical calculations and equations processed by the App are done locally on your device.

2. Data Usage

If you choose to create an account to sync your history across devices, we securely store your equations using industry-standard encryption. We do not sell, rent, or share this data with any third parties.

3. Local Storage

The application uses local storage (or browser storage if using the extension) to save your settings, preferences, and recent calculation history for quick access.

4. External Links

Our app may contain links to external sites (such as educational resources). We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content or privacy policies of any third-party sites.
